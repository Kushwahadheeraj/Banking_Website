e-Payment of Customs Duty

Punjab National Bank provides e-Payment facility of Customs Duty. Customers having a valid Import Export Code (IEC) and Internet Banking account with Punjab National Bank can make use of this facility and make online payments to Customs.

Steps for Payment using PNB Internet Banking Services(for Core Banking Branches)

  • Customer with valid Import Export Code (IEC) logs into the website of Customs i.e. www.icegate.gov.in (i.e Indian Customs & Excise Gateway).
  • Clicks on e-payment option.
  • Enters the user-id and password.
  • Selects e-payment option.
  • Selects the challan to be paid.
  • Choose PUNJAB NATIONAL BANK for making payment. ( as per EDI location ).
  • Redirected to banks website.
  • Login screen of Punjab National Bank appears.
  • Logs into bank's website with valid user-id & password.
  • Selects account to be debited - CANNOT Change Credit A/c and clicks Pay for payment.
  • Validates payment by valid userid & transaction password.
  • System confirms on status of transaction (Successful/Failure)- customer has option to print cyber receipt.
  • Returns to Customs website to complete the payment cycle.
  • Taken back to originating website of Customs in case of successful transaction.
  • In the event of Unsuccessful/ link failure would return the customer to ICEGATE e-payment gateway page.
  • Customer again clicks and checks the status by VERIFY Option and is redirected again to Bank's website.
  • Customer has the option of completing the transaction.
  • Once the transaction is complete & successful- THE CHALLAN WILL BE DISPLAYED to customer on icegate site only.
  • Only Single User (corporate ) can do the payments.
  • If your transaction is pending, please verify your transaction after 45 minutes from the time of account debited
